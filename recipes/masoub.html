Masoub

Description

Masoub, or Masoub Malaky, is a dish popular in Yemen, Saudi Arabia and a other countries in the Middle East. It is a sweet dish usually served for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Over-ripe bananas.
  • Ground flat bread.
  • Cream.
  • Cheese.
  • Dates.
  • Honey.

Steps

  1. Peel and mash the bananas. Set aside in a medium sized bowl.
  2. Coarsely grind the bread using a food processor or meat grinder.
  3. Add the ground bread to the banana mixture and stir until combined. The ratio of banana to bread should be about 1:1,one loaf of bread (a 20 cm Arabic loaf of bread but you can use pita too )and one banana per person or per serving.
  4. Add the ghee,half of the cream and the honey and mix until fully incorporated.
  5. Taste and add more honey if needed.
  6. Transfer into your serving dish.
  7. Add your choice of toppings: raisins, almonds, cream, grated cheddar (for the lovers of salty and sweet combinations), fresh banana slices (caramelized bananas are heavenly here) or honey on top.
  8. Serve warm.
